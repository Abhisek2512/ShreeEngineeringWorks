About Shree Engineering Works

Shree Engineering Works is a leading fabrication company based in Wardha, Maharashtra, delivering high-quality engineering and metal fabrication solutions since 1995. With decades of experience and a skilled workforce, we cater to a wide range of industries including manufacturing, infrastructure, and industrial plants.

Our commitment to precision, quality, and timely delivery has made us a trusted partner for clients across India.

Our core expertise lies in:

  • Custom metal fabrication including steel, stainless steel, and aluminum
  • Structural fabrication for industrial plants and infrastructure projects
  • Precision welding, cutting, bending, and assembly
  • Manufacture of bespoke components and machinery parts

We emphasize quality control and safety in all processes, ensuring each project meets the highest standards. Whether small batch or large scale, our team supports you from design consultation to delivery.
